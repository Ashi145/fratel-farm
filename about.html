About Fratel Farm Agribusiness

We don't just give advice — we build farms that work. Meet the team dedicated to transforming Ugandan agriculture.

Who We Are

We Have One Goal — To Provide the Highest Quality Agribusiness Consultancy

Fratel Agribusiness Farm is a dedicated agricultural solutions provider specialising in farm development, livestock enterprise planning, and agribusiness consultancy. We work with farmers, investors, and institutions to support the creation of efficient, productive, and modern farm systems.

Our journey began with a simple but powerful realisation: farmers across our region were full of potential, yet limited by gaps in knowledge, planning, and access to reliable farming support. We created Fratel Agribusiness Farm to close these gaps.
